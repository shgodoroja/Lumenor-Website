Common Questions

How do I connect Safari or Chrome?

Go to Preferences → Sources and click "Safari" or "Chrome". You'll be prompted to select the browser's data folder. For Safari, select ~/Library/Safari. For Chrome, select ~/Library/Application Support/Google/Chrome.

Why can't I see my bookmarks?

Make sure you've granted folder access in Preferences → Sources. If you recently changed permissions, click the reload button or restart the app. Check that the browser you want to import from is connected.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the paywall (click any Pro feature) and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase will be restored using your Apple ID. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What's the difference between free and Pro?

Free includes basic import, search, up to 10 tags, 3 collections, and HTML export. Pro unlocks unlimited tags and collections, all Chrome profiles, advanced filters (duplicates, content type, top visits), and all export formats (CSV, JSON, Markdown, OPML).

Does Arcmark modify my bookmarks?

No. Arcmark only reads your browser data - it never modifies your bookmarks, history, or browser settings. Tags, notes, and collections you create in Arcmark are stored separately in the app's local database.

Where is my data stored?

All your data (tags, notes, collections, preferences) is stored locally on your Mac in ~/Library/Application Support/Arcmark/. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. You can view and clear this data in Preferences → Privacy.

How do I export my bookmarks?

Click the Export button in the toolbar. Free users can export to HTML. Pro users can also export to CSV, JSON, Markdown, OPML, and plain text. You can export all items or just the current selection.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Go to System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to manage or cancel. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period. Lifetime purchases never expire.
