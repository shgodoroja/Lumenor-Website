Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Arcmark ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Principle: Arcmark operates on a privacy-first, local-only model. All your bookmarks, tags, notes, and collections stay on your Mac. We do not collect, store, or transmit your browsing data to our servers.

Information We Collect

1. Browser Data (Read-Only Access)

What Arcmark Reads:

  • Safari bookmarks, history, reading list, and tabs
  • Chrome bookmarks, history, and tabs
  • Browser profile information (for multi-profile Chrome support)

How We Handle Browser Data:

  • ✅ All browser data is read locally from your Mac
  • ✅ Data is processed and indexed locally for search
  • ✅ Your tags, notes, and collections are stored locally
  • ❌ We NEVER modify your browser bookmarks or history
  • ❌ We NEVER upload your browsing data to any server
  • ❌ We NEVER have access to your browsing activity

Security-Scoped Bookmarks:

  • Arcmark uses macOS security-scoped bookmarks to access browser data
  • You explicitly grant permission via folder selection dialogs
  • You can revoke access at any time in Preferences → Sources

2. Purchase Information

We use Apple's StoreKit 2 to manage in-app purchases:

What Apple Collects:

  • Purchase transaction data (managed by Apple)
  • Subscription status for monthly/yearly plans
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • ❌ Your name or email address (unless you contact support)
  • ❌ Credit card information (handled by Apple)
  • ❌ Personal identifiable information

3. Local Data Storage

Arcmark stores the following locally on your Mac:

  • Tags you assign to bookmarks
  • Notes you add to items
  • Collections you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached favicon images

Storage Location:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/Arcmark/
  • All data stored in JSON format (human-readable)
  • None of this data leaves your Mac

4. No Analytics or Tracking

Arcmark does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Usage analytics or telemetry
  • ❌ Crash reports (beyond what Apple collects via App Store)
  • ❌ Feature usage statistics
  • ❌ Your IP address or location
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information

How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we access to:

  • Display your bookmarks, history, and tabs in a unified interface
  • Enable search across all your browser data
  • Store your tags, notes, and collections locally
  • Validate purchase status via Apple's StoreKit
  • Export your data in various formats (when you request)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. Apple (Required for macOS Apps)

  • Purpose: App distribution, in-app purchases
  • Data Shared: Managed by Apple's App Store and StoreKit
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy

We Do NOT Share:

  • ❌ Your bookmarks or browsing history
  • ❌ Your tags, notes, or collections
  • ❌ Any personal information
  • ❌ Any data with advertisers or marketers

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local-Only Storage: All your data stays on your Mac
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't sync data to any servers
  • macOS Sandbox: App runs in macOS sandbox for security
  • Security-Scoped Access: Browser access requires explicit permission
  • No Network Requests: App works completely offline (except for purchases)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: View all your data in the app
  • Export: Export your data in HTML, CSV, JSON, Markdown, or OPML
  • Delete: Clear all local data via Preferences → Privacy
  • Revoke Access: Remove browser access via Preferences → Sources
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Manage Subscriptions: Manage via Apple ID Account Settings
  • Contact Support: Email us at support@babaheights.com

Children's Privacy

Arcmark is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Since all data processing is local:

  • Your bookmarks and data never leave your Mac (no transfers)
  • Purchase transactions are handled by Apple's systems

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Providing an in-app notice for significant changes

Summary

TL;DR: Arcmark is a local-only bookmark manager. All your data stays on your Mac. We never see your bookmarks, we don't collect analytics, and we only use Apple's StoreKit for purchases. Your privacy is our priority. No accounts. No cloud. No tracking.
