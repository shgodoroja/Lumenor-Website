Common Questions

How do I create a new board?

You can create unlimited nested boards within your document. Simply use the board tool from the toolbar to add a new board to your infinite canvas.

Can I import PDFs and images?

Yes! WowBoards supports importing PDFs, images, web pages, and sketches. You can drag and drop files directly onto your canvas or use the import options from the toolbar.

How do I sync between devices?

WowBoards documents are stored in your iCloud Drive. Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled on all your devices to access your documents everywhere.

What about Mac support?

WowBoards supports Mac through Mac Catalyst. Download the iOS app and it will run natively on your Mac with full keyboard and menu bar support.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open WowBoards, go to the Buy screen (⌘⇧/ on Mac), and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase will be restored using your Apple ID.
