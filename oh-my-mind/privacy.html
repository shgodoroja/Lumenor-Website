Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how OhMyMind ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Principle: OhMyMind is a privacy-first infinite canvas note-taking app. Your documents, boards, notes, and creative work are stored locally on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud Drive. We do not collect, store, or access your content.

Information We Collect

1. Your Content (Stored Locally & iCloud)

OhMyMind stores all your data locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud Drive:

  • Documents, boards, and nested boards
  • Notes, sketches, and annotations
  • Imported PDFs, images, and web pages
  • Comments and collaborative notes
  • Custom shapes, brand kits, and templates
  • Background styles and visual preferences

Where It's Stored:

  • All content is stored using Core Data on your device
  • Documents are saved in your iCloud Drive (if enabled)
  • We never access, read, or transmit your content to our servers
  • Apple may access iCloud data according to their privacy policy

2. User Preferences (Local Storage)

We store locally on your device:

  • App settings and interface preferences
  • Toolbar configurations
  • Last opened documents
  • Background style preferences per document
  • Subscription status (managed by RevenueCat)
  • Pro feature access status

Privacy:

  • All preferences are saved in UserDefaults on your device
  • None of this data leaves your device

3. Anonymous Usage Analytics (Optional)

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics to improve the App:

What We May Collect:

  • App version and iOS version
  • Device type (iPhone, iPad, Mac)
  • Feature usage statistics (which tools are used most)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics (app launch time, responsiveness)

OhMyMind is designed with privacy as a core principle. We explicitly DO NOT collect:

  • ‚ùå No Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • ‚ùå No Account System: We don't require or store user accounts

How We Protect Your Information

Security Measures

1. Local-First Storage

  • All content stored on your device using Core Data
  • No cloud storage beyond your personal iCloud Drive
  • We have no servers that store your content

2. iCloud Security

  • Documents synced via iCloud Drive use Apple's encryption
  • End-to-end encryption for iCloud data (managed by Apple)
  • You control iCloud access through iOS Settings

3. No User Accounts

  • No username/password system
  • No authentication servers
  • Reduces risk of data breaches
