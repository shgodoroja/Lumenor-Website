Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 Oct 2025

Overview

Lumenor is a focused, offline‑friendly habit tracker for macOS. We designed it to respect your time and your privacy. We do not sell your data or run third‑party trackers.

App Data (macOS)

  • On‑device by default: Your habits, entries, folders, and preferences are stored locally on your Mac.
  • No account: Lumenor does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • Offline: The app works without an internet connection.

Permissions

  • Notifications: If you enable reminders, Lumenor asks macOS for notification permission to deliver local notifications. Notification schedules are stored on device and can be changed in System Settings or inside the app.

Crash & Diagnostics

Lumenor does not include third‑party analytics SDKs. Standard macOS diagnostics may be shared with Apple if you opted into system analytics. We do not receive personally identifiable crash logs unless you explicitly email us.

Support Emails

If you contact us at lumenorapp@babaheights.com, the content you send (e.g., screenshots, logs) is used only to help resolve your request. We retain relevant messages for support history and delete them upon reasonable request.

Purchases

Lumenor is sold via the Mac App Store. Apple processes payments; we do not receive your payment information. We may see aggregated sales reports (no personal identifiers).

Website

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not run cookies or third‑party trackers. The hosting provider may log standard HTTP telemetry (e.g., IP, User‑Agent) for security and operations.

Children

Lumenor is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can export or delete your local data at any time by removing the app and its data from your Mac. For support emails, contact us to request deletion.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product changes or legal requirements. We will update the date at the top when we do.

Contact

Questions? Email us at lumenorapp@babaheights.com.
