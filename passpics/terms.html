2. App Description

PassPics is an iOS application that generates compliant passport and visa photos for various countries. The App processes photos locally on your device using machine learning to validate facial requirements, crop photos to official dimensions, remove backgrounds, and export high-resolution images suitable for printing and digital submission.

The App is developed and operated by Stefan Godoroja.
