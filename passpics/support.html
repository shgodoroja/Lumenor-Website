Common Questions

What documents does PassPics support?

PassPics supports passport, visa, ID card, and driver license photo sizes for multiple countries. Select your country and photo type to see the exact size.

How do I get a compliant photo?

Use good lighting, face the camera, keep a neutral expression, and remove glasses. PassPics auto-crops to the correct size when you tap Create.

Where do I find my purchased photos?

After purchase, your full-quality photo is saved in History. You can also share or export it from the app.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Purchase screen and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase will be restored using your Apple ID.

Why was my photo rejected?

Requirements can be strict and vary by country. If you were rejected, send us your details and we'll help diagnose the issue.
