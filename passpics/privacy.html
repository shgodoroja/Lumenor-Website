Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PassPics ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Principle: PassPics operates on a privacy-first model. All photo processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos or personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

Information We Collect

1. Photos and Images

What Happens to Your Photos:

  • ✅ All photo processing happens locally on your iPhone/iPad
  • ✅ Photos are processed using on-device machine learning (Vision framework, CoreML)
  • ✅ Processed photos are saved to your device's Photos library
  • ✅ Purchase history photos are stored locally on your device only
  • ❌ We NEVER upload your photos to any server or cloud storage
  • ❌ We NEVER have access to your photos

How We Process Photos:

  • Face detection and facial landmark analysis (on-device using Apple's Vision framework)
  • Background removal and replacement (on-device using CoreML)
  • Image cropping and resizing (on-device)
  • High-resolution export at 600 DPI (on-device)
  • Watermark application and removal (on-device)

Your Privacy is Guaranteed:

  • We have zero visibility into what photos you process
  • We cannot see your face, your identity documents, or any personal information in your photos
  • All processing is entirely local - your photos never leave your device

2. Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases:

What RevenueCat Collects:

  • Anonymous user identifier (no email or name required)
  • Purchase transaction data (managed by Apple)
  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • ❌ Your name or email address (unless you contact support)
  • ❌ Credit card information (handled by Apple)
  • ❌ Personal identifiable information

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

3. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics to improve the App:

What We Collect:

  • Country selections (which countries are most popular)
  • Document type selections (passport, visa, etc.)
  • Feature usage (compliance validation, background removal, print sheets)
  • Error logs and crash reports (no photos included)
  • App version and iOS version
  • Processing success/failure rates

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • ❌ Your photos or any image data
  • ❌ Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • ❌ Your location or IP address
  • ❌ Facial recognition data or biometrics
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information

Why We Collect This:

  • To identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • To understand which features are most used
  • To improve photo processing accuracy
  • To prioritize country requirements updates
  • To measure app performance

4. User Preferences

We store locally on your device:

  • Last selected country and document type
  • Paper size preferences (A4, Letter, 4×6, etc.)
  • Purchase history and processed photos (stored in your device's local database)
  • App settings and preferences

Where It's Stored:

  • All preferences saved in device's local storage (UserDefaults)
  • Photos saved in your Photos library or app's local storage
  • Purchase receipts stored via Apple's StoreKit
  • None of this data leaves your device

How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we collect to:

  • Process your photos locally on your device
  • Validate payment and purchase status via RevenueCat
  • Improve app stability and fix bugs
  • Understand feature usage to prioritize development
  • Provide customer support when you contact us

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. Apple (Required for iOS Apps)

  • Purpose: App distribution, in-app purchases
  • Data Shared: Managed by Apple's App Store
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy

2. RevenueCat

  • Purpose: In-app purchase management and analytics
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, purchase transactions, device info
  • Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

We Do NOT Share:

  • ❌ Your photos or image data
  • ❌ Your personal information
  • ❌ Your biometric data
  • ❌ Any data with advertisers or marketers

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • On-Device Processing: All photo processing happens locally using Apple's secure frameworks
  • No Cloud Storage: Your photos never leave your device
  • iOS Keychain: Sensitive data stored using iOS Keychain (encrypted)
  • HTTPS: All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's absolutely necessary

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Review your purchase history in the app
  • Delete: Delete photos from purchase history at any time
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Manage Subscriptions: Manage via Apple ID Account Settings
  • Contact Support: Email us at support@babaheights.com for questions

Children's Privacy

PassPics is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Since all photo processing is local and we collect minimal analytics:

  • Your photos never leave your device (no transfers)
  • Anonymous analytics may be transmitted to servers in the United States
  • RevenueCat may transfer purchase data internationally (see their privacy policy)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Providing an in-app notice for significant changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted.
