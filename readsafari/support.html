Common Questions

How do I restore a subscription?

Open the Parent Area, go to Subscription, then tap “Restore Purchases”. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase.

Why can’t my child open the paywall?

ReadSafari uses a parental gate. Subscription actions are intended for parents only.

Audio sounds robotic or mispronounced

Some sounds may use text-to-speech depending on your device. If you notice a pronunciation issue, tell us the word/sound and we’ll improve it.

How do I reset progress?

Contact us and tell us what you want to reset (streak, stars, or everything). We’ll also add a self-serve reset option if needed.

What ages is ReadSafari for?

ReadSafari is designed for early readers (Pre‑K and Kindergarten) with short daily missions.
