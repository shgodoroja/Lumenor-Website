Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ReadSafari ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information. ReadSafari is built for kids and designed for parents. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

Key Principles

  • No ads: ReadSafari does not show third-party ads.
  • No tracking: We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No account: There is no sign-in required.
  • Kid-safe purchasing: Purchase actions are behind a parental gate.

Information We Collect

1. Learning Progress (On Device)

ReadSafari stores progress locally on your device, such as:

  • Completed missions
  • Stars, streaks, and learning settings (e.g., calm mode, letter spacing)

Where it’s stored: On-device local storage (e.g., UserDefaults). We do not upload this progress to our servers.

2. Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store (StoreKit). We do not receive your payment details.

3. Support Communications

If you email us for support, we receive the information you provide (such as your email address and message). We use it only to respond and provide support.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No contact lists
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers
  • ❌ No third-party tracking or profiling

Children’s Privacy

ReadSafari is designed for children and used under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact support@babaheights.com and we will work with you to delete it.

Third Parties

We use the following third party:

  • Apple App Store: App distribution and in-app purchases (Apple Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above when changes are made.

Contact

If you have questions, email support@babaheights.com.
