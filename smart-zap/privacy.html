Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SmartZap ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Principle: SmartZap operates on a privacy-first model. All TV control happens locally on your network. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

Information We Collect

1. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the App:

What We Collect:

  • Feature usage (which buttons/commands are used most)
  • Connection success/failure rates by TV brand
  • Discovery time and pairing success rates
  • Command latency measurements
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • App version and iOS version

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • ‚ùå Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • ‚ùå Your IP address or location data
  • ‚ùå What content you watch on your TV
  • ‚ùå TV viewing history or channel preferences
  • ‚ùå Specific TV commands you send (we only track types of commands, not details)

Why We Collect This:

  • To identify and fix bugs
  • To understand which features are most used
  • To improve connection reliability for specific TV brands
  • To measure app performance and user experience

2. User Preferences

We store locally on your device:

  • Navigation mode preferences (D-pad vs touchpad)
  • Sensitivity settings for controls
  • Last connected TV device information
  • Custom macro configurations (Premium users)
  • Subscription status (via Apple's StoreKit)

Where It's Stored:

  • All preferences are saved in your device's local storage (UserDefaults)
  • TV pairing credentials are securely stored in iOS Keychain
  • None of this data leaves your device

3. TV Device Metadata

When you connect to a TV, we store locally on your device:

  • TV brand (Samsung, LG, Roku, Fire TV)
  • TV model identifier (if provided by TV)
  • IP address on your local network
  • Pairing tokens/credentials (encrypted in Keychain)
  • Connection status and quality metrics

Important:

  • This data is only used for connecting to and controlling your TV
  • It never leaves your local network
  • We do not send TV data to our servers

Information We DO NOT Collect

SmartZap is designed with privacy as a core principle. We explicitly DO NOT collect:

  • ‚ùå No Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • ‚ùå No Account System: We don't require or store user accounts
  • ‚ùå No Location Data: We don't access GPS or location services
  • ‚ùå No Viewing History: We don't track what you watch on TV
  • ‚ùå No Contact Access: We don't access your contacts or photos
  • ‚ùå No Background Activity: We only operate when you open the App
  • ‚ùå No Third-Party Data Sharing: We don't sell or share your data

How We Use Your Information

  • Usage Analytics (Anonymous): Improve app stability and performance, prioritize features, identify bugs
  • User Preferences (Local Storage): Remember your settings, auto-reconnect to your TV
  • TV Device Metadata (Local Storage): Establish and maintain connection to your TV

How We Protect Your Information

Security Measures

1. Local Network Only

  • All TV control happens on your local Wi-Fi network
  • No commands are relayed through external servers
  • No cloud storage of TV data or credentials

2. Encrypted Storage

  • TV pairing credentials stored in iOS Keychain (AES-256 encryption)
  • Secure by default using Apple's security frameworks

3. No User Accounts

  • No username/password system
  • No server-side storage of user data
  • Reduces attack surface for data breaches

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit

We use Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases:

  • Apple processes all payment information
  • We never see your credit card or payment details
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple
  • For Apple's privacy policy, visit: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

No Other Third Parties

We do NOT use:

  • ‚ùå Third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ‚ùå Advertising networks
  • ‚ùå Social media SDKs
  • ‚ùå Crash reporting services that collect PII
  • ‚ùå Any third-party data brokers

Children's Privacy

SmartZap does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

Your Privacy Rights

Data Access

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access. All your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device in the App.

Data Deletion

To delete all SmartZap data:

  1. Delete the App from your iOS device
  2. This removes all locally stored preferences, TV pairings, and settings
  3. If you have an active subscription, cancel it through Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not collect personal information as defined by CCPA
  • You have the right to request deletion of data (delete the app to remove all local data)

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union:

  • We do not process personal data as defined by GDPR
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not transfer data outside your device or region
  • You can delete all data by deleting the app

Permissions We Request

Required Permissions

1. Local Network Access

  • Why: Required to discover and connect to TVs on your Wi-Fi network
  • What We Access: Ability to scan for devices on your local network (mDNS/SSDP)
  • Privacy: All communication stays on your local network

Optional Permissions (Premium Features)

2. Microphone Access

  • Why: For voice-to-text search feature (Premium users only)
  • When Requested: Only when you tap "Voice to Text" button
  • Privacy: Speech recognition happens on-device, we never store or transmit audio

3. Speech Recognition

  • Why: To convert your voice to text for TV search (Premium users only)
  • Privacy: Uses Apple's on-device speech recognition

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored indefinitely on your device until you delete the app
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics retained for 90 days for analysis
  • Subscription Data: Managed by Apple, not stored by us

Our Promise: SmartZap will always prioritize your privacy. TV control is a utility, not a data collection opportunity.
